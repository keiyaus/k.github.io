Humidity



In the above chart, cities' humidity is plotted against their respective latitudes. In general, there are more cities with higher humidity (e.g. > 40%) in our sample. That said, cities on different latitudes exhibit a wide range of humidity. On the other hand, there are also cities with 100% or close to 100% humidity that fall on various latitudes. There does not seem to be a strong relationship between latitude and humidty. There might be other factors contributing to a location's humidity such as proximity to the sea, which are not reflected in this chart.
